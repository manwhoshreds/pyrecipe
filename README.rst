Pesto

Dish Type: sauce
Prep time: 10 m
Ready in: 10 m
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Ingredients:
3 ounces parmesan cheese, freshly grated
3 garlic cloves, peeled
2 cups fresh basil, tightly packed
1/4 cup pine nuts
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup olive oil

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Method:
1. Place cheese and garlic in a food processor and chop for
   about 30 seconds.
2. Add remaining ingredients except oil and process unitl
   combined.
3. With the machine running, pour oil through feed tube and
   process until smooth.
