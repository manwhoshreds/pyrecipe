Tzatziki Sauce

PREP 10-15 MINS
SERVES 4-6

@method
Peel the cucumber, leaving a few narrow
strips unpeeled for color, and cut in half
crosswise. Cut each piece in half lengthwise
and scoop out the seeds with a teaspoon
Discard the seeds. Finely Chop the
cucumber and place in a colander.

Sprinkle one generous teaspoon of salt
over the cucumber and mix in. Let
drain for at least 20 minutes and rinse
thoroughly. Press down with the palm of
your hands and pat dry with paper towels.

Place the yogurt in a shallow bowl, stir
in the oil and garlic, and fold in the
cucumber. Stir in the dill or mint, season
to taste, and add the lemon juice. You can
refrigerate for up to 30 minutes. Let stand
for 10 minutes before serving.
@end
